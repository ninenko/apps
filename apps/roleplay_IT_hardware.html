🎭 IT Consultant Roleplay

English for IT Students - IUCA

📋 Instructions

Your Role: You are an IT consultant helping clients choose the right computer.

  • Choose one client to consult with
  • Ask questions to understand their needs (budget, software, portability, etc.)
  • Explain hardware concepts in clear, simple English they can understand
  • Recommend appropriate components within their budget
  • Justify your recommendations by connecting features to their specific needs
  • Use technical vocabulary from class materials appropriately
  • Maintain professional communication throughout the consultation
