# TfK — Posters, slides, social, event materials

(Assumes the kit's `CLAUDE.md` core rules.)

## Signature look (default for social tiles, covers, title slides)

- Full-bleed pale green `#99AF82` field (subtle paper texture welcome).
- WHITE lockup top-left (`logo/svg|png/lockup-white`), aligned to left edge.
- Headline: huge WHITE Q3 DIN Condensed, UPPERCASE, left-aligned, can bleed off the edge (e.g. «О НАС»).
- Body/supporting text: white Calibri, small, left-aligned block.
- Accent: clusters of yellow `#FFC607` squares in pixel/staircase arrangements at corners or edges. Squares are the only decoration — no other ornaments, icons or clipart.
- Alternative scheme: white field, black logo + black text, yellow squares. Photos: white logo over a calm area of the photo.

## Brandbook backgrounds (alternative to the green field)

- Official patterns (`patterns/` SVG tiles): spruce-mark watermark (light gray on white), mint+beige diagonal stripes, dark-gray stripes, vertical mark rows. Quiet, light, never under body text.
- Natural photo textures: spruce branches, frost, stone/concrete, sand, paper — as banner strips or cover backgrounds, monochrome logo over a calm area.

## Layout discipline

Square modular grid (module ≈ the emblem); whole number of modules; logo and text flush LEFT. Simplest composition wins. Partner logos, when required, sit in a strip along the bottom edge.

## Formats

| Format | Specs |
|---|---|
| Poster A3 / leaflet A4 | Headline 24–72 pt; body 11–18 pt (Calibri); partner strip bottom; photo background allowed (see photo rules below); A1/A2 use the same grid |
| Slides (16:9) | Title slide = signature look; content slides white with black text, Q3 DIN Condensed uppercase titles; one idea per slide |
| Instagram / Facebook tile | Signature look; minimum elements; headline readable at thumbnail size |
| Badge 65×95 mm | Composition in thirds; name Calibri (Bold) 18–24, lowercase styling as in brandbook; role/event text 11–13 |
| Invitation / postcard 200×90 mm (folded) | Front: heading Calibri Bold 20–25; inside text 11–14; signatures 14–16; embossing/textured paper allowed |
| Table card 200×100 mm | Name Calibri Bold 20–25; title/affiliation 14–16 |
| Banner / press-wall | Mark repeats or single large mark; black/white logo only; green or white field |

## Photo & illustration rules (short)

Relevant to the message; convey understanding, activity, moderate academic optimism. AVOID: decorative mountains/lakes/children's faces, ecology/tourism vibes, seminar "talking heads", image overload. Rights must be cleared. Full rules: `guides/brand-core.md`.
